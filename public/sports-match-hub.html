How to use this Hub

  1. Sport — Football is the governed sport in UI2.
  2. Competition — Narrow fixtures by league or cup.
  3. Date — Select Today through Day 8 in the rolling SAST window.
  4. View — Filter by lifecycle state such as Listed or Under Review.
  5. Funnel — See how fixtures progress through governed review stages.
  6. Movement — Daily counters for admissions, moves, holds, and outcomes.
  7. Fixtures — Inspect kickoff, stage, state, and plain-language status.
  8. EdgeMind BOT — Static mock explanation for the selected day only.
