# SPM-001 — Unified Subscription Model Proposal

## Status

PROPOSED — FUTURE MINI-PROJECT — DO NOT START

## Purpose

Replace the overlapping SKCS Core and SKCS Elite subscription structures with one clear subscription ladder containing approximately four to six plans that customers can understand quickly.

## Proposed ladder

- Preview — free access for discovery and trust-building
- Sprint — 4-day entry plan
- Plus — 9-day extended plan
- Pro — 14-day regular-user plan
- Unlimited — 30-day full-access plan
- Optional VIP — introduced only when it offers materially different value

## Required decisions

- Final plan names and prices
- Access duration and activation rules
- Daily and weekly insight allocations
- Direct, Analytical, Double Chance, Same Match, ACCA, and Mega ACCA entitlements
- Chatbot request allowances
- Pro-rata access rules
- Renewal and expiry behavior
- Payment methods and failed-payment handling
- Responsible-use language
- Refund, cancellation, and consumer-rights wording
- Whether access is recurring or fixed-term

## Product integrity law

Edge must never manufacture low-confidence predictions to satisfy a numerical subscription allocation. All advertised allocations must be expressed as maximum available access, subject to event availability, data quality, confidence thresholds, sport-specific rules, and SKCS safety controls.

## Customer experience objective

The customer should see one simple progression:

Try SKCS → use it for a few days → upgrade for longer access → become a monthly member.

Detailed daily allocation tables should be available in a comparison view rather than overwhelming the initial plan-selection screen.

## Hold conditions

SPM-001 must remain queued until product entitlements, payment-provider capability, access-control enforcement, regional pricing, consumer-law requirements, and legal terms have been separately approved.

## Definition of Done

One governed subscription model exists with clear pricing, duration, entitlements, upgrade paths, expiry rules, payment behavior, customer disclosures, and enforceable access controls, with no conflict between marketing promises and Edge confidence safeguards.

## Scope protection

This proposal does not activate billing, process payments, change current entitlements, alter prediction output, or disturb the current active mini-project.
